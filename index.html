Resumé of Dr. Andrew R. Tait

Entrepreneur | Scientist | Community Member

Summary

Dr. Tait is the founder of Tait Laboratories Inc., a company focused on modernizing traditional herbal medicines. With a strong academic background in biochemistry and chemistry, including a Ph.D. in Chemistry, he has been recognized for his entrepreneurial achievements and expertise in natural health products, with numerous speaking engagements and awards. His work involves collaborating with researchers and organizations to develop safe and clinically proven medicines, including his successful launch of MS+ Mandarin Skin Plus®, a digestive product made using patented extraction technology he co-invented. He is passionate about upcycling and unlocking the highest value from overlooked materials.
